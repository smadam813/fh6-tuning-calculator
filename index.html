Recommended Tune

Handling bias Neutral (0)
← Understeer Oversteer →

Balance. Tilts the tune toward front grip (understeer) or rear rotation (oversteer) by moving each end the opposite way — ARB, springs, differential, brake balance & aero. At 0 it's the pure per-goal baseline.

Overall stiffness Balanced (0)
← Soft Hard →

Firmness. Scales springs, anti-roll bars & damping the same way at both ends (and drops/raises ride height) — so the car gets harder or softer without changing its balance. At 0 it's the pure per-goal baseline.
